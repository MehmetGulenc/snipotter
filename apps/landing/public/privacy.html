Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Snipotter is a clipboard manager that syncs your clipboard history across your devices. To provide this service, we collect:

  • Clipboard content: Text, URLs, and other data you copy to your clipboard
  • Notes: Quick notes you create within the app
  • Device information: Device type and identifier for sync purposes
  • Authentication data: Email and authentication tokens to secure your account

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Sync your clipboard history across your devices
  • Store and organize your notes
  • Authenticate and secure your account
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Supabase: Our backend database provider, which encrypts data at rest
  • End-to-end encryption: Clipboard content is encrypted during transmission
  • Secure authentication: We use industry-standard authentication methods

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Retention

Clipboard history is retained according to your account settings (default: 100 items). You can:

  • Delete individual clipboard items
  • Clear your entire clipboard history
  • Delete your account and all associated data

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • GitHub: For distribution of desktop applications

These services have their own privacy policies and are committed to protecting your data.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Export your data
  • Opt out of data collection

7. Children's Privacy

Snipotter is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
